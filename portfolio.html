Crack Restaurant
Client Crack Restaurant
Industry Restaurant · Food & Beverage
Services Social Media · Content Creation
The Challenge
  • Inconsistent posting and a visual identity that didn't reflect the energy of the restaurant
  • Low engagement across Instagram and TikTok despite a loyal in-house following
  • No content engine in place to turn daily restaurant life into scroll-stopping social posts
What We Offered
  • A full social media strategy and content calendar built around the menu, atmosphere and customers
  • On-site filming days to capture brand films and short-form video on a recurring schedule
  • Hands-on community management to grow engagement and turn followers into regulars
What We Delivered
  • Weekly cinematic brand films and reels showcasing dishes, behind-the-scenes moments and the in-store vibe
  • A consistent visual identity and tone of voice carried across every post and story
  • Always-on community management — replying, engaging and building a loyal social following
Results
+265% Growth in monthly reach
3.8x Increase in engagement rate
+180% Growth in video / reel views
12K New followers in 4 months
